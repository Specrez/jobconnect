Job Title: Senior Software Engineer

Location: Colombo, Sri Lanka (Hybrid)

Department: Technology

Job Summary

As a Senior Software Engineer, you will work closely with the engineering team to develop high-quality software solutions and applications that solve complex problems for our clients.

Key Responsibilities

  • Design, develop, and maintain scalable software solutions.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to deliver high-quality products.
  • Mentor junior engineers and contribute to their growth.
  • Participate in code reviews and ensure the best engineering practices.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field.
  • 5+ years of experience in software development.
  • Proficient in JavaScript, Node.js, and React.
  • Experience with cloud platforms like AWS or Azure is a plus.

Preferred Qualifications

  • Master's degree in a related field.
  • Experience with containerization and microservices.
  • Excellent communication and teamwork skills.

Working Conditions

Hybrid work model with flexible hours. Some on-site collaboration may be required.

Salary & Benefits

  • Competitive salary based on experience.
  • Health insurance and retirement benefits.
  • Paid vacation and sick leave.
  • Opportunities for career growth and skill development.
